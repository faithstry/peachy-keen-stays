Questions

Frequently Asked

What states do you work in? +
We offer virtual design services in all 50 states, and in-person staging in select markets. Reach out to ask about your area!
How long does the design process take? +
Virtual design typically takes 3–4 weeks from kickoff to final delivery. Full installation projects run 4–8 weeks depending on scope and location.
How does property management work? +
We sign a corporate lease on your property and become your tenant. You get guaranteed on-time rent, we handle guest management and most maintenance under $200.
What's included in virtual design? +
A custom mood board, full room-by-room design plan, curated shopping list with direct links, before/after renders, and 30 days of post-launch support.
Do I need to be local for the design service? +
Not at all! Our virtual design is fully remote — you get everything delivered digitally and can execute it yourself or with a local team.
