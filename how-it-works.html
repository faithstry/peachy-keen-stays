Service 03

Rental Arbitrage

No property? No problem. We identify, negotiate, and launch short-term rental arbitrage deals — leasing long-term and subletting short-term. You own the business, not the building.

01

Market Analysis

We identify the highest-performing submarkets for arbitrage in your target city — by occupancy, ADR, and competition.

02

Deal Sourcing

We find and vet properties that meet our criteria: layout, price, landlord flexibility, and STR potential.

03

Landlord Negotiation

We handle the pitch and lease negotiation, including STR-friendly addendums that protect you and the landlord.

04

Design & Launch

We design, furnish, and launch the property just like our full-service design clients — guest-ready in weeks.

05

Operations Setup

Cleaning teams, smart locks, pricing tools, guest messaging — everything automated before your first booking.

06

Ongoing Co-Hosting

We stay on to manage the property so you can scale to multiple units without adding hours to your week.
